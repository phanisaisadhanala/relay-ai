RELAY SETTINGS & COORDINATION
  - Distance relay zone reach: Z_sec = Z_primary × (CTR / PTR)
  - Zone 1: 80–85% of protected line impedance (instantaneous, no delay)
  - Zone 2: 120% of protected line (0.3–0.4 s delay)
  - Zone 3: 220% of protected line (0.6–1.0 s delay)
  - IDMT overcurrent: t = TMS × K / ((I/Iset)^α − 1)
      IEC Standard Inverse : K=0.14,  α=0.02
      IEC Very Inverse      : K=13.5,  α=1.0
      IEC Extremely Inverse : K=80.0,  α=2.0
  - Differential protection operate condition: |Id| > k × |Ibias| + I_min
      Slope 1: 20–30%    Slope 2: 50–60%
  - CT knee-point voltage: Vk > If_max × (RCT + Rleads)
  - Source Impedance Ratio: SIR = Zsource / Zline

FAULT ANALYSIS — SEQUENCE NETWORKS
